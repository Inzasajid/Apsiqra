Mission of APS Iqra

Army Public School & College (Iqra) in Risalpur Cantt aspires to be a leading educational institution that nurtures excellence in academics, character, and leadership. Our vision is to empower students with a robust educational foundation, equipping them with the skills and values necessary to thrive in a rapidly changing world. We are dedicated to creating an inclusive and inspiring learning environment that encourages curiosity, critical thinking, and innovation.
Through our comprehensive facilities and diverse programs, APS&C Iqra aims to develop well-rounded individuals who contribute positively to society and lead with integrity and resilience.
