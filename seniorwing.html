Welcome to APS Iqra Senior Wing

At APS Iqra Senior Wing, we are committed to providing a nurturing environment that blends rigorous academic standards with a holistic approach to student development. Our focus extends beyond traditional education to include the cultivation of leadership, innovation, and global awareness, ensuring our students are well-prepared for the future.

Our Educational Philosophy

We believe that education is a transformative journey, and our mission is to guide students through this process with dedication and passion. Our philosophy emphasizes:

  • Academic Excellence: Delivering a challenging curriculum that promotes intellectual growth and critical thinking.
  • Personal Development: Encouraging self-discovery, resilience, and emotional intelligence through tailored support and mentorship.
  • Global Perspective: Preparing students to thrive in a diverse world by integrating international viewpoints and fostering cross-cultural understanding.
  • Community Engagement: Building a sense of responsibility and active citizenship through involvement in community service and collaborative projects.

Core Values That Shape Our Community

  • Curiosity: Sparking a love for learning and discovery that drives academic and personal growth.
  • Courage: Empowering students to face challenges with confidence and perseverance.
  • Integrity: Upholding the highest standards of honesty and ethical behavior.
  • Pride: Fostering a strong sense of achievement and respect for oneself and others.

Academic Programs

At APS Iqra, we offer a diverse range of academic programs designed to cater to students' interests and career aspirations. Our school exclusively provides education under the Federal Board of Intermediate and Secondary Education (FBISE), ensuring a standardized and recognized curriculum. Our academic offerings include:

  • Matriculation: Students can choose from Biology and Computer Science, allowing them to explore their interests and build a strong foundation for future studies.
  • FSC (Higher Secondary School Certificate): We provide three focused streams:
    • Pre-Medical: For students interested in pursuing careers in medicine and healthcare.
    • Pre-Engineering: Tailored for students aiming to enter the fields of engineering and technology.
    • ICS (Intermediate in Computer Science): Designed for those who wish to specialize in computer science and information technology.

Our comprehensive academic offerings are designed to cater to a wide range of interests and career goals, providing students with the knowledge and skills necessary to excel in their chosen fields. We are dedicated to supporting each student’s unique journey and preparing them for success in a rapidly evolving world.
