Welcome to APS Iqra Junior Wing

At APS Iqra Junior Wing, we ignite a passion for learning and personal growth by nurturing our students' spiritual, moral, social, and cultural development. Our approach seamlessly integrates core values into every aspect of school life, creating an environment where young minds thrive.

Our Core Values

  • Curiosity: Encouraging a spirit of inquiry and exploration.
  • Courage: Building resilience and bravery to face challenges.
  • Integrity: Promoting honesty and strong ethical principles.
  • Pride: Instilling a sense of accomplishment and respect for oneself and others.

Together, these values guide our students as they embark on their educational journey, shaping their character and preparing them for a future of success and fulfillment.
