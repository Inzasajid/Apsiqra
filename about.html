About Us

Army Public School & College (Iqra) in Risalpur Cantt is a prestigious educational institution managed by the Army Public Schools & Colleges Secretariat. Renowned for delivering high-quality education, it serves students from Risalpur Cantt and nearby regions. The school is affiliated with the Federal Board of Intermediate & Secondary Education (FBISE) in Islamabad.
APS&C (Iqra) offers exceptional facilities, including well-equipped labs for physics, chemistry, and biology, as well as a computer lab, a library, an auditorium, and recreational areas. Catering to students at the elementary, secondary, and higher secondary levels, APS&C Risalpur Cantt is committed to fostering a holistic learning environment.

About Us

Innovative Learning Environments

At APS Iqra, we embrace the future of education by integrating cutting-edge technology with traditional teaching methods. Our modern facilities and interactive platforms provide an immersive learning experience that fosters creativity and critical thinking. Students at APS Iqra are equipped with the tools and skills to excel in an ever-evolving world, making learning both engaging and effective.

Global Perspectives

APS Iqra prides itself on its diverse and inclusive community, where students from various cultural backgrounds come together. This multicultural environment enriches the academic experience by fostering global awareness and cross-cultural understanding. By interacting with peers from around the world, students gain valuable insights and prepare for success in a globalized society.

Empowering Future Leaders

At APS Iqra, we focus on developing the leaders of tomorrow through a holistic approach to education. Our students are encouraged to participate in a wide range of co-curricular activities and experiential learning opportunities. This comprehensive approach helps them build leadership skills, resilience, and innovation, preparing them to make a significant impact in their chosen fields and beyond.
